Notre Histoire

Traditional French Cuisine with a Family Touch

Rooted in the heart of the 15th Arrondissement, Le Dôme Paris 15 is more than a restaurant; it is a legacy of culinary passion. For decades, we have served as a sanctuary for those seeking the soul of authentic French bistro culture.

Our 4.8-star reputation is built on the unwavering commitment of our family to source only the finest local ingredients. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the weight of heritage and the warmth of a home-cooked meal, refined for the modern palate.
